What is Equipment Financing?

Equipment financing allows you to purchase or lease business-critical equipment through monthly payments rather than paying the full cost upfront. The equipment itself serves as collateral, often making approval easier and rates more competitive than unsecured loans.

Finance vs. Lease: Which is Right for You?

Equipment Financing (Loan): You own the equipment at the end of the term. Better for equipment you'll use long-term and that retains value. Monthly payments typically build equity.

Equipment Leasing: Lower monthly payments, equipment returned or purchased at end of lease. Ideal for technology that becomes obsolete quickly or when you want to upgrade regularly. May offer tax advantages.

Benefits of Equipment Financing

  • Preserve Cash Flow: Keep working capital for operations instead of large upfront equipment purchases
  • Tax Advantages: Potential Section 179 deductions and depreciation benefits (consult your accountant)
  • 100% Financing Available: Many lenders offer no down payment options
  • Easy Approval: Equipment as collateral typically means faster approval than unsecured loans
  • Flexible Terms: Match payment terms to equipment's useful life and your cash flow
