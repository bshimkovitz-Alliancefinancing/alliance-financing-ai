What is SR&ED Financing?

Scientific Research & Experimental Development (SR&ED) financing lets you access your tax credits immediately, without waiting for the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).

Bridge Your Cash Flow Gap

If your company is conducting scientific research or experimental development work in Canada, you may be eligible for significant SR&ED tax credits. Historically, businesses had to wait for the CRA to process their claims—sometimes 12–18 months or longer.

SR&ED financing changes that. We provide bridge financing against your anticipated refund, giving you cash when you need it most.

How It Works

A straightforward process from claim to cash.

1
File SR&ED Claim

Your company files an SR&ED claim with the CRA, documenting your qualifying R&D work and expenses.

2
Apply for Financing

Submit your application along with your filed claim and documentation. We'll review and provide a quote within 1–2 business days.

3
Get Funded

Upon approval, receive up to 70–80% of your expected refund. Funds land in your account typically within 2–5 business days.

4
Repay from Refund

When the CRA issues your refund, we deduct the financing amount plus fees and return the balance to you.
