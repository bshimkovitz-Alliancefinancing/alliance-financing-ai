PRIVACY POLICY

Your Privacy Matters

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

Alliance Financing Group collects various types of information to provide our commercial financing services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Business Information: Company name, revenue, years in operation, and industry sector
  • Financial Information: Bank statements, tax returns, and credit information

This information is collected through financing applications, portal registration, and ongoing account management.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to support and enhance our financing services:

  • Process and evaluate your financing applications
  • Match your business with appropriate lenders from our network of 70+ partners
  • Manage your client portal account and access
  • Communicate with you regarding your applications and our available products
  • Improve our AI matching platform and service offerings

3. Information Sharing

We share your information only as necessary to process your financing requests. Alliance Financing Group does not sell your personal data.

Your information may be shared with:

  • Lender Partners: Only the information necessary for underwriting and lending decisions
  • Credit Bureaus: For credit checks and verification as part of the application process
  • Service Providers: Vendors who support our platform hosting, email services, and data security
  • Legal and Regulatory: When required by law or regulatory authorities

4. Data Security

Alliance Financing Group takes data security seriously and implements industry-leading protections:

  • Bank-grade encryption for all data transmission and storage
  • Secure document vault with access controls
  • Compliance with PIPEDA (Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act)
  • SOC 2 security practices and regular security audits

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information held by Alliance Financing Group
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Request data portability in a structured format

To exercise these rights, please contact our privacy team at privacy@alliancefinancing.ai.

6. Cookies and Analytics

Alliance Financing Group uses standard analytics and session cookies to understand how users interact with our platform. These cookies help us improve your experience and platform functionality. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes via email and through notifications in your client portal. Your continued use of our services following such notice constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Alliance Financing Group Ltd.
  • Toronto, Ontario, Canada
  • Email: privacy@alliancefinancing.ai
  • Phone: 1-877-660-3660

9. Canadian Privacy Compliance

Alliance Financing Group is committed to compliance with the Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA) and all applicable Canadian federal and provincial privacy legislation. We handle personal information in accordance with these regulations and maintain appropriate safeguards to protect your privacy rights.
