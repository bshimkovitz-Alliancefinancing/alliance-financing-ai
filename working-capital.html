What is Working Capital Financing?

Working capital financing provides your business with immediate access to funds to cover short-term operational expenses and investments. Whether you need to manage cash flow gaps, purchase inventory, meet payroll, or fund expansion, working capital solutions offer the flexibility and speed your growing business demands.

Common uses for working capital:

  • Purchasing inventory or raw materials
  • Managing seasonal fluctuations in cash flow
  • Meeting payroll and employee benefits
  • Funding marketing and customer acquisition
  • Expanding operations or opening new locations
  • Bridging gaps between accounts receivable
  • Equipment upgrades or technology investments
