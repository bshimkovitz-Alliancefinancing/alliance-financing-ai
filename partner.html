Frequently Asked Questions

What is the commission structure?
+
Alliance pays 1-2% referral fees on the funded deal value. For example, a $500,000 equipment financing deal generates $8,000–$15,000 in referral income. Commissions are calculated monthly and paid by the first business day of each month for deals approved and funded in the previous month.
What are the four partnership channels?
+
Direct Applications (SEO-optimized website with AI-powered credit applications), Referral Partners (mortgage brokers, insurance agents, accountants, financial advisors), ISO / Agent Network (independent sales organizations with CRM and pipeline management), and Vendor Programs (equipment dealers and service providers offering financing at point of sale with white-label options).
Is there a cost to start?
+
No. Zero cost to start — no platform fees, no setup costs, no minimum volume requirements. You can refer as few as one deal and still earn commissions. Begin earning immediately with no financial commitment.
How fast are deals processed?
+
Most deals receive responses within 48 hours. Standard approval is 24-48 hours, and approved deals fund within 1-2 business days. All processing and lender negotiation is handled by Alliance — you just refer and collect commissions.
Can I use a white-label / branded portal?
+
Yes. Choose between Branded Programs (custom portal on your domain with white-label lender network) or Non-Branded Referral (simple model where Alliance handles everything). Branded programs offer higher revenue per deal but require more setup. Non-Branded is ideal for fast launch.
What financing products do you offer?
+
Equipment leasing, working capital financing, merchant cash advances (MCA), commercial mortgages, and more across 70+ lender relationships. AI-powered matching finds the best product and lender for every deal automatically.
How do I track commissions?
+
All partners get access to a real-time portal showing live deal pipeline, commission tracking on every funded deal, and detailed monthly commission statements. Transparent fee visibility and deal status updates are included.
Do I get dedicated support?
+
Yes. Each partner is assigned a dedicated Alliance advisor who handles all referred client calls and questions. White-glove support is included — we want you and your clients to succeed.
