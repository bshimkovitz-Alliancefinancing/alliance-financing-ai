CAPEX VS OPEX

Protect Your Borrowing Capacity

Leasing equipment preserves your cash flow and maintains financial flexibility. Unlike purchasing, a lease is treated as an operating expense (OPEX) rather than a capital asset, keeping your balance sheet lean and your credit lines available for growth.

100% tax-deductible as an operating expense. Flexible terms with technology refresh cycles. Off-balance-sheet treatment protects bank credit facilities. No impact on your debt ratios or borrowing capacity. Scale your operations without tapping your line of credit.
