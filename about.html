OUR STORY

Founded on Experience, Built on Innovation

Since 1989, Alliance Financing Group has been at the forefront of commercial financing in Canada and the United States. What started as a commitment to bridge the gap between businesses seeking capital and lenders seeking opportunity has evolved into a comprehensive financing platform serving thousands of clients.

Our journey has been defined by innovation, reliability, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' success. We've built deep relationships with over 70 lender partners and funded more than $1 billion in commercial transactions.

Today, with an 85% approval rate and response times under 48 hours, Alliance Financing Group continues to set the standard for speed, transparency, and customer-centric service in commercial financing.

OUR PLATFORM

Technology That Powers Success

Alliance Financing Group has invested heavily in building a proprietary platform that combines industry expertise with cutting-edge artificial intelligence. Our in-house technology stack includes:

AI Credit Applications

Intelligent application processing that learns from thousands of transactions, reducing time-to-decision and improving accuracy.

Deal Pipeline CRM

Comprehensive tracking system that keeps all stakeholders informed and coordinated throughout the entire financing lifecycle.

Document Management

Secure, organized system for handling all documentation requirements with automatic compliance checking and archival.

Partner Portals

Dedicated interfaces for lenders, agents, and vendors to manage their relationships and track opportunities in real-time.

Notification System

Intelligent alerts keep all parties informed of status updates, deadlines, and opportunities without information overload.

Marketing Engine

Data-driven tools that help partners reach qualified businesses and manage their marketing campaigns effectively.
