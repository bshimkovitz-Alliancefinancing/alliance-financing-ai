Our Story

Alliance Financing Group Ltd. was founded with a simple belief: every business deserves access to the right capital at the right time. Over 35 years, we've built deep relationships across the lending industry — from major banks to specialty finance companies — and helped thousands of businesses secure the funding they need to grow.

As the financing landscape evolved, so did we. We recognized that traditional broker models left businesses waiting weeks for answers, navigating confusing paperwork, and settling for suboptimal terms. That's why we built Alliance Financing AI — a platform that combines our extensive lender network with artificial intelligence to match businesses with qualified lenders in as little as 48 hours.

Today, we serve businesses across North America, from startups needing their first line of credit to established companies pursuing multi-million dollar acquisitions. Our AI analyzes each application across hundreds of data points to identify the best-fit lenders, while our experienced team provides the personal guidance that technology alone can't replace.
