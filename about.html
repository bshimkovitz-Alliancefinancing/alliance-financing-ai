Our Story

Founded in 1989, Alliance Financing Group has been at the forefront of commercial financing in Canada for over three decades. What began as a vision to simplify business financing has evolved into a comprehensive financial solutions platform serving thousands of Canadian enterprises.

We are a syndication desk — we don't lend our own money. Instead, we leverage our extensive network of 70+ institutional lenders to find the optimal financing solution for each client. This unique position allows us to negotiate competitive rates and terms that might not be available through traditional channels.

Our expertise spans equipment leasing, working capital financing, and specialized solutions like SR&ED financing. With over a billion dollars in cumulative funding facilitated, we've built a reputation for reliability, transparency, and client-focused solutions.

At Alliance, we believe that financing shouldn't be complicated. Our team works directly with Canadian businesses to understand their unique needs and match them with the right lender, at the right terms, at the right time.

Regulatory Compliance

Alliance Financing Group is a designated reporting entity under Canada's Financial Transactions and Reports Analysis Centre (FINTRAC) and complies with the Proceeds of Crime (Money Laundering) and Terrorist Financing Act (PCMLTFA).

As of April 2025, we maintain full compliance with all federal regulations governing commercial finance intermediaries. This designation reinforces our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of integrity and security for our clients and partners.
