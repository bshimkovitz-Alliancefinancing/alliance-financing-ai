Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our financing solutions and services.

Alliance Financing Group is a commercial finance brokerage established in 1989. We are NOT a bank. Instead, we specialize in matching businesses with optimal financing solutions by shopping your deal across our network of 70+ institutional lenders. This gives you access to a wider range of products and competitive terms than you would typically get from a single financial institution.

As a brokerage and syndication desk, we shop your financing deal across 70+ lenders to find the best terms and structure for your business. Unlike a bank—which offers only its own products at its own rates—we function as your advocate in the lending marketplace. We leverage relationships across multiple institutions, alternative lenders, and specialized funding sources to secure better rates, higher approval odds, and more flexible terms tailored to your specific needs.

We serve all industries across Canada and the United States. Whether you're in manufacturing, retail, healthcare, professional services, technology, construction, or any other sector, our team has experience structuring financing solutions. Our breadth of lender relationships and product lines means we can find funding for most legitimate business needs, regardless of your industry vertical.

We offer 9 product lines to address different business needs:

1. Equipment Leasing – Finance equipment without owning it
2. Working Capital – Quick access to cash for operations
3. MCA (Merchant Cash Advance) – Repay from daily sales
4. SR&ED Financing – Government R&D tax credit advances
5. Invoice Factoring – Convert outstanding invoices to cash
6. Private Debt – Flexible lending for growth
7. Commercial Mortgage – Real estate and property financing
8. SBA Loans – U.S. Small Business Administration loans
9. Trade Finance – Import/export and cross-border solutions

Borrowing capacity ranges from $10,000 to $50 million and beyond, depending on the product and your business profile. Smaller businesses typically qualify for equipment leases and working capital in the $10K–$500K range, while established companies may access commercial mortgages, private debt, and syndicated solutions in the multi-million dollar range. The exact amount depends on factors like revenue, cash flow, creditworthiness, and the specific use of funds.

Requirements vary by product, but general guidelines include:

• Business Age: Minimum 6+ months in operation (some products accept newer businesses)
• Revenue Thresholds: Varies by product; typically $150K–$500K+ annual revenue
• Documentation: Recent business financials, bank statements (3–12 months), personal and corporate tax returns, and a brief description of your financing needs
• Credit: While we work with businesses of varying credit profiles, stronger credit typically yields better terms
• Collateral: Some products require collateral; others do not

Contact us for a personalized assessment of your eligibility.

Our typical timeline is:

• Initial Response: Within 48 hours of application submission
• Full Approval: 3–10 business days depending on product complexity and document completeness

Equipment leases and working capital are typically faster, while mortgages and syndicated private debt may require additional underwriting. We'll keep you updated throughout the process and flag any missing documentation early.

To streamline your application, please have these documents ready:

• Business Financials: Last 2 years of financial statements (income statement, balance sheet)
• Bank Statements: Last 3–12 months (depends on product)
• Tax Returns: Personal and corporate tax returns for last 2 years
• Financial Needs Summary: A brief description of what you need to finance and how it supports your business
• Proof of Ownership/Business Registration: Articles of incorporation, ownership certificates, or business licenses

Our team can advise on additional requirements based on your specific product and situation.
