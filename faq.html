FAQ

Everything You Need to Know

Find answers to common questions about Alliance Financing Group and our services

General

What is Alliance Financing Group? ▼
Alliance is Canada's technology-driven commercial financing platform. Since 1989, we've connected businesses with the right financing through a network of 70+ lenders across Canada and the U.S. Our AI-powered platform matches your business with optimal lenders — delivering decisions in 24-48 hours, not weeks.
How is Alliance different from a bank? ▼
Banks offer their own products with their own criteria. Alliance gives you access to 70+ lenders through a single application. Our AI matches your deal to the best options across our entire network. 85% approval rate vs. the 20-30% you'd typically see at a single bank.
What industries do you serve? ▼
We serve businesses across all industries — technology, manufacturing, construction, healthcare, professional services, retail, transportation, and more. If your business needs capital, we can help.

Application Process

How do I apply? ▼
Submit an application online at alliancefinancing.ai or through one of our referral partners. The process takes less than 10 minutes. You'll need basic business info, financing amount, and purpose.
How long does approval take? ▼
Most decisions are made within 24-48 hours. Our AI-powered lender matching runs instantly — the remaining time is for lender underwriting and documentation.
What documents do I need? ▼
Typically: 2 years of financial statements or tax returns, 3 months of bank statements, a brief description of the financing need. Requirements vary by product and amount.
What credit score do I need? ▼
We work with all credit profiles. Our 70+ lender network includes options for strong credit, rebuilding credit, and everything in between. The AI matching ensures you're presented to lenders most likely to approve your specific profile.

Products

What financing products do you offer? ▼
9 product lines: Equipment Leasing ($20K-$5M+), Working Capital (MCA, lines of credit, business loans), SR&ED Financing (advance up to 80% of your tax credit), Commercial Mortgages, Factoring, Trade Finance, Private Debt, and Business Loans.
What's the difference between leasing and buying equipment? ▼
Leasing preserves cash flow and is 100% tax-deductible as an operating expense. It keeps equipment off your balance sheet, protects bank credit facilities, and includes technology refresh options. Buying ties up capital and sits on your balance sheet.
What is SR&ED financing? ▼
SR&ED (Scientific Research & Experimental Development) is a Canadian government tax credit program. Alliance advances up to 80% of your expected refund so you don't have to wait 12-18 months for the government to process your claim.

Partners

How does the partner program work? ▼
Refer your clients who need commercial financing. Alliance handles the entire process — application, lender matching, underwriting, and funding. You earn a referral commission on every funded deal. Zero cost, zero compliance burden.
What types of partners do you work with? ▼
Mortgage brokers, insurance brokers, accountants, financial advisors, equipment vendors, ISO agents, and any professional with business clients who need financing.
Do you offer a white-label / branded program? ▼
Yes. Partners can choose between a branded program (financing presented under your brand, powered by Alliance) or a standard referral model (clients work directly with Alliance). Both earn commissions.
