Property Types We Finance

  • Office Buildings: Single-tenant and multi-tenant office spaces
  • Retail Properties: Shopping centers, standalone retail, restaurants

  • Special Purpose: Hotels, medical buildings, self-storage

Types of CRE Financing

Purchase Loans: Finance 70-90% of property purchase price for owner-occupied or investment properties.

Refinancing: Lower your rate, pull out equity, or consolidate debt on existing commercial properties.

Construction Loans: Fund new construction or major renovations with draw schedules tied to project milestones.

Bridge Loans: Short-term financing while you stabilize occupancy, complete renovations, or arrange permanent financing.

Who Qualifies?

  • Property must generate income (rental or owner-occupied business)
  • Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR) of 1.20+ preferred
  • 10-25% down payment depending on property type
  • Credit score of 680+ for best rates (some programs 620+)
  • 2+ years business history (3+ years for investment properties)
  • Strong business financials or rental income history

Key Metrics Lenders Evaluate

Loan-to-Value (LTV): Most lenders finance 70-80% of property value. Higher down payment = better rates.

Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR): Property income divided by debt payments. Lenders want 1.20x+ (20% more income than debt).

Occupancy Rate: For investment properties, higher occupancy means better loan terms.

Borrower Strength: Your credit, liquidity, and business history all factor into approval and rates.

Benefits of CRE Financing

  • Build Equity: Property appreciates while tenants pay down your mortgage
  • Tax Advantages: Depreciation, interest deductions, and potential 1031 exchanges
  • Portfolio Diversification: Real estate provides income and inflation hedge
  • Control Your Space: Owner-occupied properties eliminate rent volatility
  • Leverage: Control valuable assets with relatively small down payments

Typical Rates & Terms

Interest Rates: 5-10% depending on property type, location, and borrower strength

Loan Terms: 1-30 years (often 10-25 year amortization with 5-10 year balloon)

Down Payment: 10-25% depending on property and borrower

Closing Costs: 2-5% of loan amount

Prepayment Penalty: Common in first 3-5 years (declining scale)

*Rates based on market conditions, property quality, location, and borrower creditworthiness. All financing facilitated through licensed mortgage brokers.
